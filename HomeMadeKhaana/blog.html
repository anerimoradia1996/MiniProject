5 Little 3-Minute Tasks That’ll Make Your Counters Way Less Cluttered

You know how people talk about making the bed like it’s the most important thing anyone can do when it comes to a tidy home and peace of mind? Well, I honestly believe that clean countertops are more important. The kitchen really is the heart of the home; we likely spend more of our (waking) minutes in there than in our bedrooms. So waking up to or working around cluttered counters is going to add some stress to your day — whether you realize it or not. Here are some easy things you can do to clear your counters off, and help clear your mind, in just a few minutes.

  • Put a tray under a group of items
  • Gather items that belong in other areas of the house.
  • Check your counter for items you don’t need anymore.
  • Re-home items that don’t need to be out on the counter.
  • Put your dish rack away.
